Our Mission & Vision

At ReLeaf, our mission is to reconnect people with nature by restoring green spaces through community-driven tree planting, education, and environmental stewardship. We believe that by empowering individuals to take simple, collective action, we can heal ecosystems, combat climate change, and create a healthier planet for future generations.

Our History

Founded in 2021 by a small team of environmentalists, GreenRoot began with one mission: to make tree planting easy, transparent, and impactful. With just a handful of volunteers and a lot of passion, we planted our first 500 trees in affected regions of Amazon.

Since then, we've expanded our reach across multiple continents, collaborating with local communities, conservationists, and everyday donors like you. Every campaign is a step toward restoring ecosystems and fighting climate change.

Today, with thousands of donors and over 150,000 trees planted, our roots run deeper than ever. And we're just getting started.
